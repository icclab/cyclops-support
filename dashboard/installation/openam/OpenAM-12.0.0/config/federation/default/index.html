Federated Identity
Revolutionizing the Internet





Federated Identity is all about simplifying the Internet for you. It will allow you to concentrate your distributed identity over the net.

You may have thousands of accounts at hundreds of sites. With Federated Identity in place you need to login only once at only one place to get access to all the information you need.
